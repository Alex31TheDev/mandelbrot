Viewport

  • Left click uses the current mode.
  • With Seed Point or Light Point, left click samples from the image.
  • Right click zooms out in zoom modes.
  • In Pan mode, right click also pans.
  • Middle drag, or hold Space, for temporary pan.
  • Mouse wheel zooms, resizes a zoom box, or changes iterations by 10 in Pan mode.
  • Realtime Zoom zooms continuously while held.
  • Box Zoom lets you drag a target rectangle.
  • Hold Shift for slower motion and Ctrl for faster panning.
