Help / Controls

Controls

  • Render: backend, iterations, color method, fractal, Julia, inverse.
  • Info: center, zoom, seed, plus save/load view.
  • Viewport: mode, pick target, pan speed, zoom speed, exponent.
  • Sine: preset, RGB frequencies, randomize, import, save.
  • Palette: preset, import, save, edit, length, offset.
  • Light: light position and light color.
  • Image: width, height, anti-aliasing, aspect lock, viewport resize.
