Files and Recovery

  • Saved views store fractal type, flags, iterations, center, zoom, and seed.
  • Unsaved sine and palette edits are marked in their combo boxes.
  • Switching presets can ask you to discard local edits.
  • Unsaved sine and palette edits are auto-recovered on the next start.
  • If the current view changed, closing can prompt you to save it.
